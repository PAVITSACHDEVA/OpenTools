About DocFix

DocFix is a simple, privacy-friendly toolkit for preparing PDFs and images for fast, reliable uploads.

What we solve

  • Compress PDFs that exceed size limits.
  • Convert and optimize images for form submissions.
  • Create quick visuals without installing extra apps.

Built with care

DocFix is part of the OpenTools initiative, focused on creating small, trustworthy utilities that work directly in your browser.
